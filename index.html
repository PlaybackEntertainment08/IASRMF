About IASRM

The highest result of education is tolerance

The International Association of Stem Cell & Regenerative Medicine (IASRM ) is an independent, non-profit organization that aims to support and raise advancement in stem cell science and its applications to human health. This organization has been established to bring together researchers, clinicians, health professionals, and companies interested in Stem Cells and Regenerative Medicine.

IASRM is essential to Scientists/ Doctors/ Researchers/ Entrepreneurs/ Academia/ Medtech companies / Industries, working in Regenerative Medicine, Stem Cell, Tissue Engineering, translational medicine and gene & Cell based therapies.

IASRM is open to all those who have manifested a continuous interest in any discipline important to stem cell research, personalised medicine, Individualome, and regenerative medicine as evidenced by work in the field, original contribution and attendance at meeting concerning stem cell research and therapeutics.

Join IASRM

Why Join IASRM

Modern lifestyle and emergence of numerous pathological diseases have degraded the capacity of the human body to cure and regenerate itself this led to the discovery of the amazing benefits of stem cell technology.

Today, stem cells represent an exciting area in medicine because of their potential to regenerate and repair damaged tissue. Some current therapies, such as bone marrow transplantation, already make use of stem cells and their potential for regeneration of damaged tissues.

IASRM is the worlds leading community for stem cell research and education. By joining IASRM, you get access to all the latest information about stem cell technology.

Education & Professional Development

IASRM offers the most extensive library of information on stem cell, cellular therapy, and regenerative medicine. The educational process continues outside where members also learn from each other via professional discussion forums, local meet-ups, and global events.

Development of Leadership Skills

Members may get the opportunity to guide new members, participate in the research community or play a global role at IASRM. This adds to their professional experience and can increase their chances to get a leadership role in the industry.

Contribution to Stem Cell Industry

Proven experts in the field can contribute to the quality of stem cell education and professional services. IASRM offers outstanding publishing opportunities to its members to reach its captive audience in the scientific community.

Advancing Research & Practices

IASRM is a global platform for collaboration and alliances for Translational Research, Cell and Gene Therapy, Tissue Engineering, Immuno, & Developmental Biology
