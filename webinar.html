HealthTAPE

Health TAPE is an Academic and Scientic initiative by International Association of Stemcell and Regenerative Medicine (IASRM) and StemMax Centre of Advance Research (SCAR) to Recognize Global Health Heroes in their respective area of expertise.

A platform where experts from their elds share the knowledge of the eld and talk about their success and life journey. The goal of health Tape is to educate and inspire not only the professionals but the webinars are tailored for general public as well to stay informed of the latest advancements in regenerative medicine and stem cell therapy.

Why Health Tape?

A platform where experts from their elds share the knowledge of the eld and talk about their success and life journey. The goal of health Tape is to educate and inspire not only the professionals but the webinars are tailored for general public as well to stay informed of the latest advancements in regenerative medicine and stem cell therapy.

Weekly Sessionss

Every week we shall have a global speaker talk about their expertise and modern techniques, followed by an interview session moderated by doctors and scientists, which will be broadcast- ed on IASRM social media pages (Facebook, Youtube, Zoom).
