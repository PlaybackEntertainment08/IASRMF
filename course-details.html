Masterclass in Cosmetic Gynaecology

Course Description

A comprehensive theoretical & practical learning of invasive & non-invasive regenerative functional aesthetic gynaecology. Course will cover majorly energy based devices, prp, stemcells, carboxy therapies, botulinum toxins, ha filler, threads, etc.

What You’ll Learn From This Course
Theory & Practical’s / Hands-on for following Procedures:
  • Energy Based Devices (Laser, Radio-frequency, HIFU), Botulinum Toxins, Fillers, PRP, Fat Grafting, Stem cells, Threads, HIFEM.
  • Video Based Procedures for Labiapalsty , Hymenoplasty, Labia Majora Augmentation, Perennioplasty and other Surgical Procedures.
  • Regenerative Medicine, Functional & Aesthetic Medicine advancing Women’s Intimate Health.
  • Learn How & Where to use Laser , Radio frequency, HIFEM, And Platelet Rich Plasma in intimate well-being.
  • You will learn A-Z Of EBD & PRP along with inclusion , exclusion criteria, how to set up aesthetic gynaecology centre.
  • Indications from Vaginal Atrophy , GSM, Dyspareunia, Stress urinary incontinence , Interstitial Cystitis, Lichen Sclerosus, Vaginal Rejuvenation by PRP , Orgasm Enhancement, Stretch Marks.
  • Vaginal tightening and vaginal rejuvenation treatments with EBD.
  • Improvement of the sexual pleasure
  • Facilitation of the orgasmic function
  • Elimination of the menopausal complaints
  • Treatment of vaginal dryness
  • Treatment of recurrent vaginal infections
  • Treatment of recurrent vaginal fungal infections.
